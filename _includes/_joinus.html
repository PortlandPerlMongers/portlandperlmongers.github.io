Join Us

PDX.pm is a support and educational group that meets to discuss the Perl language and its ecosystem. We would love to have you join us. Membership or prior Perl experience is not required, all meetings are free-of-charge and all are welcome.

Meetings are on the second Thursday of each month at 6:53pm. Most meetings are held at Free Geek . After the meeting, some tend to walk over to the Lucky Lab  for post meeting drinks and food.
